CORONAVIRUS DISEASE

HOW TO PREVENT?

Clean Your Hands

Clean your hands regularly either using an alcohol-based hand wash or if it is not avilable, then use soap and water to wash. This can kill the virus in your hand.

Don't Touch Your Face

Avoid touching your face especially eyes, nose and mouth. Hands may be contaminated with virus as it touches surfaces.

1 metre distance

Maintain 1metre distance from other people to avoid liquid droplets coming out when someone speak, cough or sneeze.

No Crowded Place

Avoid crowded place since you are more likely to come in contact with Covid-19 positive person.

Stay Home

Avoiding going out of your home to prevent contamination with virus from outside.

Going Out

Step out of your home only if it is absolutely necessary. Keep atleast 1m distance from others.

Once home, wash your hands and the things with you thoroughly.

Inform Health Officials

If any kind of symptoms appear, then immediately inform local health autority and also avoid close contact with your family. Thus you will get right directions regarding health and also prevent spread of virus. Before visiting, always call the health officials.

Follow NEWS

Be updated with latest coronavirus news. Avoid rumors. Follow news from WHO and your local health authority.

WHAT ARE SYMPTOMS?

On an average it takes up 5-6 days to show symptoms. But in some cases it may take 14 days. Seek medical attention if any sort of symptoms appear.

Most Common Symptoms:

  • Tiredness
  • Fever
  • Dry cough

Less common symptoms:

  • Sore throat
  • Diarrhoea
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • A rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes
  • Aches and pains

WHAT IS CORONAVIRUS?

Corona virus disease is an infectious disease. It is spread mainly through saliva as well as discharge from nose.The disease comes from coronavirus. The disease was first reported in China and now it has spread to almost all countries. China identified it as a new type of coronavirus.

People affected with coronavirus will experience respiratory problems. Most of the people recover without any special treatment. But for older people and for people with other medical problems like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer have more chance of having serious illness.
